About Goodwill Co.

Established in 1999, Goodwill Co., is a leading safe company in Vietnam, specializing in research and development the safe products for domestic and foreign market such as fire resistant safes, hotel safes, deposit safes, and others security products. At the same time, Goodwill is one of the largest export company and plays an important role in the Safe and security industry in Vietnam with the modern manufacturing factory located in the South of Vietnam, 20 minutes from the center of Ho Chi Minh City.

 

Goodwill improves continuously its technology, improves the qualification of the engineers and workers, the ability of management as well as the flexibility in the sales and the good service after sales. All that brings us a system of the firm retail distribution from North to South of Vietnam and other countries after more than 10 years of development.

 

With the slogan "Our Safe Your Well", Goodwill is confident to become the largest Safe Company in Vietnam
